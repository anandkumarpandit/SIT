Welcome to STI

Sagarmatha Technical Institute (STI) is a premier CTEVT-affiliated technical college dedicated to providing quality education and practical training in various technical fields. Located in Kathmandu, Nepal, we have been shaping futures and building careers through excellence in technical education.

Our institution focuses on hands-on learning, industry-relevant curriculum, and student-centered approach to education. We combine theoretical knowledge with practical skills to prepare our students for successful careers in their chosen fields.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a leading technical education institution in Nepal, recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and producing skilled professionals who contribute to national development.

Mission

  • Provide quality technical education aligned with industry needs
  • Develop skilled and competent technical professionals
  • Promote practical learning and innovation
  • Create an inclusive and supportive learning environment
  • Contribute to the socio-economic development of Nepal

Admission Process

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Academic certificates (SLC/SEE or equivalent)
  • Citizenship certificate or birth certificate
  • Recent passport-sized photographs (4 copies)
  • Character certificate from previous institution
  • Migration certificate (if applicable)

Admission Steps

  1. Fill out the application form (online or offline)
  2. Submit required documents
  3. Appear for entrance examination (if applicable)
  4. Attend counseling session
  5. Pay admission fee
  6. Complete enrollment process
